Thanks For Reading

This game is a low polygon 3D art style with a medieval theme.

  • The logo is a shield with crossed swords behind it, representing the medieval theme, and the font typography made it look fun.
  • The game's user interface is flat design with a gradient because it matches the low polygon art style.
