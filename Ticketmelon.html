Ticketmelon

(Redesign)

ABOUT THE PROJECT
Ticketmelon is an ticket purchase application that allows you to buy an even ticket on your smartphone.

The goal of this solo project is to learn about app critique and

redesign as part of the Designing Interactive Systems course.

DURATION

November 2022
